Our Story

Founded in the heart of Dubai's Al Fahidi district, Noor Al-Shama began as a small workshop crafting traditional date wax candles for local mosques and households. Our name, meaning "Light of the Candle" in Arabic, reflects our deep connection to regional lighting traditions.

During the 2015 Gulf fragrance boom, we pioneered the fusion of premium paraffin wax with rare regional ingredients like Omani frankincense, Emirati saffron, and Qatari oud. This innovation earned us the 2018 Middle East Home Fragrance Award.

Today, we operate sustainable production facilities in Dubai and Jeddah, supplying over 500 luxury retailers across the GCC while maintaining our commitment to traditional craftsmanship.
